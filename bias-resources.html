Understanding Impicit Bias

What is Implicit Bias?

Implicit Bias is the unconscious actions, stereotypes, or thoughts that change the way individuals behave. Implicit Bias refers to involuntary behaviors that take place without us noticing. This is important to be aware of and understand as discrimination can take place due to these biases. Here it can be better explained:

The Implicit Bias Test (IBT)

The Implicit Bias Test, is a psychological test created at Harvard that measures the automatic associations between groups and how you feel about them unconsciously. After taking the tests it places you into groups that either showed that you were more or less likely to be biased than the rest of the participants of the test.

Understanding Intersectionality

Intersectionallity is how a different person's race, gender, class, and sexuality that changes how they view and are treated in society. It's important as it understands other people's thoughts and actions based on that viewpoint.
